Blackberry Care

Sunlight

  • 6-8 hours

When to Plant

  • Plant when canes are dormant, Early spring preferrably.

Planting Recommendations

  • Erect blackberry bushes are self supporting while trailing blackberries
    require trellis support.
  • Pruning is important to keep bush under control.

Soil

  • pH: 6.2-6.8 for best nutrient absorption
  • Mix in 3-4 inches of compost

Water

  • Water regurlarly. At least an inch of moisture per week
  • If soil is dry, it's time to water
