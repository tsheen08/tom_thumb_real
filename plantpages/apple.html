Apple Care

Sunlight

  • 6+ hours

When to Plant

  • Spring

Planting Recommendations

  • Ensure soil is well draining.
  • Clear 4 ft diameter circle of grass and weeds.
  • 15-18 feet apart.

Soil

  • pH: 6.0-6.5 for best nutrient absorption
  • Mix in 3-4 inches of compost

Water

  • Water regurlarly. At least an inch of moisture per week
  • If soil is dry, it's time to water
