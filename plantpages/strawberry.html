Strawberry Care

Sunlight

  • 8+ hours

When to Plant

  • Spring/Fall planting

Planting Recommendations

  • Use of compost is recommended
  • Can be grown in ground, pot or raised bed.

Soil

  • pH: 5.5-6.8 for best nutrient absorption

Water

  • Water regurlarly. At least 1-1.5 inches of moisture per week
  • avoid wetting leaves.
