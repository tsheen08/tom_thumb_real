My Garden

Gardening Tip 1 / 3
You can buy compost at any Garden supply store
You can also create your own with Kitchen scraps. Avoid meat dairy or
citrus products. Coffee grounds and eggshells are also great additions
Gardening Tip 2 / 3
Make sure your soil is draining properly and not too dense
plants and vegetables need room to grown. If your soil is too dense or stays
wet too long, they can suffocate and die.
Gardening Tip 3 / 3
Need a helping hand? Look into beneficial bugs for your garden!
earthworms, ladybugs and bees are great to have around. These bugs can help break
down soil, pollinate plants, or eat harmful pests!
